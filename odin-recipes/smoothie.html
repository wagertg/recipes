Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 c. frozen strawberries
  • 1 c. frozen blackberries, plus more for garnish (optional)
  • 1 c. frozen raspberries
  • 1 1/4 c. almond milk
  • 1/2 c. Greek yogurt

Description

A smoothie is so much more than blended fruit. It's an art with all of its different elements blending perfectly together.

Recipe credits:

  • Delish!

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine all ingredients and blend until smooth.
  2. Divide between 2 cups and top with blackberries, if desired.
